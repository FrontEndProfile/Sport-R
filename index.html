About Us

Sporting Risk leverages the predictive modelling of its data science team with a vast tech operation to generate pricing for one of the largest betting syndicates in the UK. The predictive modelling is powered by the most extensive and granular data available, internally collected by the company’s tech team and qualitative analysts.

This proprietary data is also used to power the pricing for Providers and Operators in the gaming sector, as well as creating engaging product and content offerings, servicing businesses in gaming, media and professional sport.

The company’s data collection and predictive modelling originated in football. In recent years this has extended into US sports, meaning the company now holds an extensive pricing, product and content portfolio across a range of sports.
